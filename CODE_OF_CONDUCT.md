# Code of Conduct

We want discussions to stay practical, respectful, and useful for people evaluating or operating Hermes Agent.

## Expected Behavior

- Stay on topic.
- Assume public comments may be read by new users.
- Provide evidence when correcting technical claims.
- Keep private account, payment, and credential details out of public threads.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Posting secrets or private user data
- Spam or unrelated promotion
- Repeated off-topic arguments after maintainers ask to stop

Maintainers may edit, hide, or close content that does not fit these guidelines.
